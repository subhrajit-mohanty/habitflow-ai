## Full Description (copy below)
----

HabitFlow AI is your personal habit coach that uses artificial intelligence to help you build lasting micro-habits.

Unlike generic reminder apps, HabitFlow learns YOUR patterns — analyzing when you're most productive, energized, and likely to complete each habit. The result? Smart notifications at the perfect moment, not just another alarm you'll ignore.

KEY FEATURES:

AI Smart Scheduling — Our ML engine analyzes your daily patterns to find the optimal 2-minute window for each habit. The more you use HabitFlow, the smarter it gets.

Conversational AI Coach — Chat with your personal habit coach anytime. Get weekly reviews, habit stacking suggestions, and motivation based on your actual data — not generic advice.

Streak Engine — Gamified streaks with XP, levels, and 13 unlockable badges. Earn bonus XP for long streaks and celebrate milestones.

Mood & Energy Tracking — Log how you feel throughout the day and discover which habits actually boost your wellbeing. See real correlations between your habits and your mood.

Photo Check-ins — Verify habits with your camera for accountability. Our vision AI confirms completion so you stay honest.

Buddy System — Pair with friends as accountability partners. Send nudges, share streaks, and compete in community challenges.

Beautiful Analytics — Completion trends, weekly heatmaps, per-habit breakdowns, and mood-habit correlations. Understand your patterns at a glance.

START WITH JUST 2 MINUTES:

Choose from 10 science-backed micro-habits: meditation, hydration, stretching, reading, gratitude journaling, deep breathing, and more. Each takes just 2 minutes. The AI coach helps you stack habits and find the perfect routine.

PRO PLAN ($4.99/month or $39.99 lifetime):

Built for people who want real results, not just another app they'll delete in a week. Start your 2-minute habit journey today.
